About Me

I’m a Data Scientist Intern at Mercado Bitcoin and an undergraduate Data Science student at the University of São Paulo, focused on machine learning, statistical analysis, and data-driven decision-making. My work involves developing predictive models, performing statistical analyses, and transforming complex data into practical insights for business problems. I have experience with GCP, Python, SQL, PyTorch, Scikit-learn, Pandas, NumPy, SciPy, LangChain and other data science tools. I have also been involved in academic research as an undergraduate student researcher, contributing to scientific publications and working on AI research projects, including a FAPESP-funded project focused on reliability, predictive uncertainty, and robustness of deep learning models applied to medical computer vision. I’m especially interested in applied machine learning, predictive modeling, and robust AI systems. I’m also exploring GenAI, LLMs, and agentic AI, particularly their applications in automation, analytics workflows, and decision support.
