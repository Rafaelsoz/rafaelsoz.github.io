About Me

Data Scientist at Mercado Bitcoin, with a degree in Data Science from the University of São Paulo, focused on applying machine learning, statistics, and data-driven methods to solve business problems.

In my day-to-day work, I build predictive and clustering models, run causal inference analyses, and explore data to better understand business and product challenges. I enjoy working across the full data science workflow, from data exploration to model development and deployment, using cloud and analytics tools.

Core areas of expertise:

  • Machine Learning & Predictive Modeling
  • Clustering & Unsupervised Learning
  • Causal Inference
  • Statistical Analysis, including non-parametric methods
  • Python, SQL, PyTorch, Scikit-learn, Pandas, NumPy and SciPy
  • Google Cloud Platform (GCP)
  • Business Analytics & Decision Support

I also have a background in academic research, with experience contributing to scientific publications. I worked on a FAPESP-funded project focused on improving the reliability, predictive uncertainty, and robustness of deep learning models.

Currently exploring GenAI, LLMs, and agentic AI, with a focus on their applications in automation, analytics workflows, decision support, and real-world data science products.
